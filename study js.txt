
STUDIO
Beautiful model agency consists of various departments that specialize on management, promotion, modeling and photo sessions. The latter deserves special attention because of our approach to creating photo sessions and the equipment we use to reach the goal as well as implement the ideas of our customers. We pay a lot of attention to the concept of our photographers who initially discuss the whole project idea with our clients, whether be it a photo session for a fashion magazine, a billboard, a booklet or a CD cover. Our photo studio is also different from other studios, even independent ones, in ability to accomplish a set goal in undertime. 

However, we do not separate ourselves from the world of modeling and photography, and try to correspond to the latest trends in fashion and photography. Our photographers take part in the leading exhibitions dedicated to the world's problems – from hunger in Africa to global warming and war. We do not limit ourselves to fashion only, our photographers gladly participate in various conferences and exhibitions as guests. One of the main reasons of our success is devotion of our photo studio members. They are fully dedicated to what they do and from day to day they create highly anticipated content that satisfies the needs of even the most exacting clients.
